Codi utilitzat per l'experiment del TFM: Aligning Credit Scoring with the EU AI Act: Bias Mitigation Techniques in Financial Machine Learning.
Abstract
The deployment of machine learning algorithms in retail banking has transformed credit risk assessment. 
However, proprietary, opaque predictive systems raise significant concerns regarding transparency, algorithmic bias, and regulatory compliance.
This work aims to dissect the implications the EU AI Act will have on credit scoring models and analyze the trade-offs between profitability and fairness that take place 
when applying different bias mitigation techniques in a realistic credit scoring setting.
